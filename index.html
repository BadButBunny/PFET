Your Exercises

Kegel Exercises

Contract your pelvic muscles for 5 seconds, relax for 5 seconds. Repeat 10 times.

Pelvic Tilts

Tighten abs, press lower back to floor for 5 seconds. Repeat 10 times.

Bird-Dog

Extend arm and opposite leg, hold for 10 seconds. Repeat 5 times per side.

Heel Slides

Slide one heel away, hold 5 seconds, repeat 5 times per leg.
